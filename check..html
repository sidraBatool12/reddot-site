Relocating your home is also one of life’s important milestones as it symbolizes success, upgrade or even regrets. Like organizing any important event, finding a reliable moving partner to celebrate this life journey is essential. Reddot Movers is proud to have played her part in thousands of such life’s journeys. Over the years, we have come to understand that every move has it’s own unique story and requirements and we have the experience and skill to deliver the best experience you might ever have. You can choose between just the basic service where we will deliver you our carton boxes and other necessary packing materials for you to pack your personal and loose items, or you can choose our full service where our movers will take care of every detail.

Service Levels

We are flexible in providing our customers customizable service levels to cater to you affordability, convenience and satisfaction.

You can pre-pack loose items and your own personal effects in carton boxes delivered by us and our movers will take care of the rest – Truck, professional labour, wrapping service and some simple disassembly of furniture.
